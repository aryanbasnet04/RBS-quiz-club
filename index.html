Welcome to RBS QUIZ CLUB

RBS QUIZ CLUB is the club formed for the overall development of the student of Rato Bangala School. The club aims to improve the general knowledge, Maths and IQ of the students using the best technique and methods. Our club aims to help students identify what they know and what they don't know. It motivates students to study more and helping them allocate their study time effectively. It also promotes a fun way to study and in the process help improve one's general knowledge.


-Aryan Basnet, President of RBS QUIZ CLUB

We believe in creating a nurturing environment which cultivates critically thinking and innovative lifelong learners who embrace and adapt to challenges, thrive in an ever-changing world and grow into compassionate socially commited and open-minded global citizens.

-Rato Bangala School Mission
